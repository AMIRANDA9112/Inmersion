About us

Get to know a bit more about Project Immersion and the team behind it.

A lot has changed in 2020.

Due to the covid-19 global pandemic, businesses across virtually every industry have been forced to change their 'status quo'. Basic tasks, such as meetings and presentations, are no longer conducted in face-to-face formats. Therefore the need for video-chat platforms has incresed dramatically.

Augmented reality tools as a plugin.

But for many people, the need to see a face talking while someone presents their slides is very important to keep engaged in the presentation. Likewise, for the presenter to be able to stand up and use his hands may be very useful at the moment of explaining something that requires the whiteboard. These are the main advantages of using our plugin for video-chat platforms.
